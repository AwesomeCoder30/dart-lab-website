# Project Brief

## Organization

DART Lab is a student-led policy think tank focused on:

- drug pricing
- healthcare access
- real-world policy impact

Its work includes:

- policy briefs
- research
- op-eds
- collaboration with academics, policymakers, and organizations
- student pathways into serious research and publication

## Why The Site Matters

The website is used as an external credibility layer for:

- professors and researchers
- policy professionals
- institutional partners
- university leadership
- student recruits

The bar is not "good for a student group." The bar is "credible enough to support funding, partnerships, and serious institutional conversations."

## Outcome Required

The site should feel:

- professional
- structured
- credible
- readable
- intentional

It should not feel:

- startup-like
- overdesigned
- flashy
- marketing-heavy
- cluttered

## Design Direction

Primary inspiration: the White House website.

Target traits:

- restrained palette
- strong typography
- simple hierarchy
- generous whitespace
- consistent button language
- clean navigation

## Technical Constraints

- final production site will be built in Webflow
- must support CMS collections for `Team Members` and `Publications`
- fully responsive on desktop and mobile
- fast-loading and easy to navigate

## Top Navigation

The site should contain exactly these seven top-level destinations:

1. Home
2. Publications
3. Programs
4. Team
5. About
6. Get Involved
7. Contact

Main navigation should stay clean. No cluttered dropdown structure in the primary menu.
